### Can security guards sleep on the job?
No, a security guard must not sleep on the job. They are hired to ensure the security of the premises and should not doze off under any circumstances. If the guard feels drowsy, they should inform their supervisor so that alternate personnel can be arranged as soon as possible.

### What is a security guard not allowed to do?
Security guards are not allowed to:

- Engage in any activities that are not related to their job duties
- Use excessive force or violence
- Hold People or vehicles without any due reason
- Enter any premises without permission
- Discriminate against anyone based on race, religion, gender, or other protected characteristics
- Carry a weapon without proper authorization
