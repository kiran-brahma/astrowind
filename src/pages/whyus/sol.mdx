## Service Delivery

At Knighthood, our Service Delivery procedures are specifically designed to meet the needs of each Customer. We make sure to keep our Customers in the loop, and keep them informed at every step of the Service Delivery process.

## Periodic Reviews

At Knighthood, we believe in the importance of periodic reviews with Customers to ensure a constant feedback loop in our Service delivery. This helps us to make sure we are meeting our Customer's needs and expectations, and allows us to make adjustments as needed.
